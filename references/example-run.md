# Example Run

This is a demonstration of what a strong output from this skill should look like.

## Scenario

- Product: Portable desktop cable organizer
- Audience: Office workers and students
- Price: 29-49 RMB
- Goal: Click-through to ecommerce listing
- Primary platform: Xiaohongshu
- Secondary platform: Douyin
- Vertical: Home / Cleaning / Storage

## 1. Offer Decision

- Selected angle: "Cleaner desk in 10 seconds"
- Why this angle wins:
  - Easy visual proof
  - Broad pain point
  - Strong save/share potential

## 2. Platform Plan

### Xiaohongshu

- Goal: Search discovery and save-worthy note
- Content type: Practical recommendation note
- CTA: "看最后一张图的使用效果"

### Douyin

- Goal: Fast visual hook and click-through
- Content type: 12-15 second problem-to-solution clip
- CTA: "点开主页看详情"

## 3. Copy Pack

### Source Offer

- Product: Portable desktop cable organizer
- Pain point: Messy charging cables and cluttered desk
- Promise: Keep common charging cables fixed and reachable
- Proof: Before/after desk setup
- CTA: View details

### Xiaohongshu Title

- 桌面线材终于不乱了，这个小东西真的省事

### Xiaohongshu Body

- 每次找充电线都要从桌边捞半天，真的很烦。
- 我最近换了这个桌面理线器，最明显的变化就是线不再掉到桌后。
- 安装很快，放在办公桌和床头都顺手。
- 适合经常充手机、耳机、手表的人。
- 我最喜欢的是拿线的时候不用弯腰找。
- 想看使用前后对比，直接看最后一张。

### Douyin Hook

- 你的桌面是不是也总被这几根线搞乱？

### Douyin Script

- Shot 1: messy cables on desk
- Shot 2: hand installing organizer
- Shot 3: clean desk and quick cable grab
- Voiceover: "一个小理线器，桌面立刻清爽很多。"
- CTA: "点主页看详细款式。"

### Ecommerce Listing Title

- 桌面理线器 免打孔收纳固定夹 充电线整理器 办公宿舍通用

### FAQ

- Q: 会不会粘不住？
- A: 适合平整干净表面，安装前先擦干。

- Q: 能放几根线？
- A: 视型号不同，适合常用充电线和数据线整理。

## 4. Browser-Ready Fields

- Product title: 桌面理线器 免打孔收纳固定夹 充电线整理器 办公宿舍通用
- Short description: 整理常用线材，让桌面更清爽，取线更顺手
- Tags: 桌面收纳, 理线器, 数据线整理, 办公桌好物
- CTA link: use active product listing URL

## 5. Risks and Blockers

- Do not claim permanent adhesion without proof
- Avoid exaggerated before/after claims
- Stop for human review before final post and publish

## 6. KPI Checklist

- Note saves
- Video completion rate
- Product click-through
- Chat inquiries
- Add-to-cart
- Orders

## How To Reuse This Example

Change only:
- Product
- Audience
- Platform pair
- CTA

Keep the structure the same for consistent operations.
